Top three activities to do in Seattle

Space Needle

The Space Needle in Seattle stands as an iconic, futuristic marvel, offering panoramic views of the cityscape from its soaring, needle-like structure. Its sleek design and rotating observation deck provide a unique and unforgettable experience for visitors.

Hikes

Seattle boasts a plethora of diverse hiking trails, ranging from lush, moss-covered forests in the Cascades to the rugged, coastal paths along Puget Sound. These hikes offer adventurers a chance to explore pristine wilderness, with opportunities to witness stunning waterfalls.

Mount Rainer

Mount Rainier, an iconic stratovolcano in the Seattle area, stands as the tallest peak in the Cascade Range, its snow-capped summit reaching 14,410 feet. This majestic mountain not only anchors the surrounding national park but also serves as a haven for mountaineers, hikers, and nature enthusiasts.
